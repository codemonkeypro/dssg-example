## About

DSSG is a **static site generator** written in the D programming language. It creates HTML pages out of *Markdown* files and *Mustache* templates.

What you can do with DSSG:
* Company websites
* Portfolio websites
* Personal websites
* Hobby websites
* And a lot of other cool stuff
